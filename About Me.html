About Me

My name is Nicholas Pendleton. I am currently an honors student at Louisiana State University pursuing a degree in Art and Design with a minor in Mass Communication. My whole life I have been enraptured by the creative process may it be music composition, painting, digital art, or videography. I decided to make a career in challenging my creative muscles to embrace the unique and rewarding life of creating.
